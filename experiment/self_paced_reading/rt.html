Thank you for participating in our study. In this study, you will read several paragraphs and sometimes answer questions about them. The HIT should take 40~50 minutes. Your patience is highly appreciated. Thanks!

We invite you to participate in a research study on language production and comprehension. Your experimenter will ask you to do a linguistic task such as reading sentences or words, naming pictures or describing scenes, making up sentences of your own, or participating in a simple language game.

There are no risks or benefits of any kind involved in this study.

You will be paid for your participation at the posted rate.

If you have read this form and have decided to participate in this experiment, please understand your participation is voluntary and you have the right to withdraw your consent or discontinue participation at any time without penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled. You have the right to refuse to do particular tasks. Your individual privacy will be maintained in all published and written data resulting from the study. You may print this form for your records.

This is how it works: read through the vignette simply by pressing the space bar. Sometimes there will be a comprehension question after the vignette that checks whether you understood the vignette or not. You can feel free to take breaks between vignettes, but please do NOT take a break when you are reading through one vignette. Please do your best to read the sentences at a natural reading speed.
